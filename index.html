Justin Welch

Founder and fine art photographer of Justin Welch Photography.

I was born in South Africa, lived for many years in the UK and am now based in Costa Rica. As a landscape, urban, portrait and travel photographer, I love to explore new destinations, both well known and off the beaten track. There is something special about slowly wandering the streets of a city or paths in the countryside, absorbing the sights and sounds from which I draw my inspiration.
